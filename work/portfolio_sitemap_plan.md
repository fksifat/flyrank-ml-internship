# Portfolio Sitemap & Claude Project Setup Guide

This document outlines the required elements for your Week 1 portfolio assignment. Since the deliverables require manual screenshots and a photo of a physical/digital sketch, use this guide to quickly complete the steps and submit them to your track thread.

## 1. Portfolio Sitemap Sketch Plan

**Your Goal:** Draw a simple sitemap (boxes and lines on paper or a digital whiteboard) showing how you guide a visitor from your main claim to a specific action.

**The Claim (Example for ML Intern):** "I build ML pipelines that surface actionable insights from messy data, helping teams prioritize efforts and avoid noise."
**The Action:** "Email me to discuss optimizing your data workflows."

### The Sitemap Structure (Keep it lean!)
*   **Home / Landing Page:**
    *   **Hero Section:** States the claim clearly (What I do + Why it matters).
    *   **Featured Work:** 1-2 key case studies (e.g., The FlyRank Refresh Opportunity Scorer).
    *   **Call to Action (CTA):** "Let's talk about your data." -> Links to Contact.
*   **Case Study Page (e.g., Refresh Scoring):**
    *   Deep dive into the ML framing, the baseline vs. model, and the actionable queue generated.
*   **About Me (Short):**
    *   Brief professional background, technical stack (Python, Pandas, ML), and working style.
*   **Contact / Action Page:**
    *   Simple form or email link to initiate the primary action.

*(Action: Sketch this out on paper or a tool like Excalidraw, take a photo, and save it for submission).*

## 2. Claude Project Configuration

**Instructions for Setup:**
1. Open Claude and create a new **Project**. Name it something like "Portfolio Build & Tutor".
2. In the **Custom Instructions** section, paste the following:

> **Custom Instructions for Claude:**
> "I am building a professional portfolio based on this core proof statement: *'I build ML pipelines that surface actionable insights from messy data, helping teams prioritize efforts and avoid noise.'* My primary goal is to get hiring managers or leads to email me for a consultation/interview.
> 
> Please act as my expert portfolio tutor and critic. Do not just agree with me. Challenge my assumptions, ensure every single element on my site serves my core claim and leads to the primary action, and point out fluff or unnecessary additions."

*(Action: Take a screenshot of this configured Project screen).*

## 3. The Pressure-Test Prompt

Run this exact prompt inside your newly created Claude Project to pressure-test your sitemap.

**Prompt to copy/paste:**
> "Here is my proposed portfolio sitemap:
> 1. Home (Hero with claim, Featured Work, CTA to Contact)
> 2. Case Study: FlyRank ML Scoring Pipeline
> 3. About Me (Background, Tech Stack)
> 4. Contact (Email form)
> 
> Pressure-test this sitemap against my core claim and my one desired action (getting an email). Is there any page or section here that doesn't earn its keep? Is there any friction preventing the user from taking action? Tell me at least one specific thing I should change or remove."

### Expected Output & Adjustment
Claude will likely respond by pointing out potential friction. 
*Example adjustment based on feedback:* Claude might suggest moving the "Contact" form directly onto the bottom of the Home and Case Study pages so the user doesn't have to click to a separate page to take action.

*(Action: Take a screenshot of the prompt and Claude's response, and note the change you will make).*
