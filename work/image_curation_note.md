# Image Curation Note for the Portfolio

## Final image set (the keepers)
1. Real screenshot of the case-study workflow or model output for the Refresh Opportunity Scorer. This should show the actual work clearly and make the portfolio feel evidence-led.
2. Real screenshot of the sitemap/portfolio-planning artifact, such as the hand-drawn or digital planning image already in the workspace, to show process and intent.
3. One calm AI-generated texture or abstract background that matches the identity kit palette and supports the hero section without competing with the work.
4. A real photo of me for the About section, because the person behind the work should be shown as a real person, not an AI stand-in.

## What I rejected and why
I rejected a flashy, overly cinematic AI image of a data scientist in a futuristic setting. It looked generic, it pulled attention away from the actual work, and it would make the portfolio feel more like an ad campaign than a thoughtful case study.

## Where I chose real capture over AI
I chose real captures for the work itself: the case-study evidence, the planning artifact, and the personal photo. I only used AI for support imagery, and only in a restrained style that matches the calm palette and tone of the identity kit.
